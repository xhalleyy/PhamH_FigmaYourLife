Satoru Gojo : The Strongest

Satoru Gojo is one of the many major charactor in the manga/ anime, Jujutsu Kaisen. Satoru strives to protect the world from the Cursed Spirits that plague it. However, Gojo believes the structure of Jujutsu Sorcerer society is flawed, and as such hopes to change it for the better. This explains why he's a teacher at Jujutsu Kaisen High School and directly mentors first year students, such as Yuuji Itadori and Megumi Fushiguro.
