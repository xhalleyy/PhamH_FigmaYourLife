Accomplishments and Powers

Satoru Gojo is the pride of the Gojo Family, since he was the first person to be born with the Limitless and Six Eyes in four hundred years. When he was born, it was said that he changed the balance of the world. Curses grew in power in response to that to balance out his powers.Even among special grade sorcerers, Satoru is known to be the strongest socercer in the series. This explains why even if he's a teacher, he can defy higher-ups, since fear going against him. Since he's able to go against them, he was able to save promising jujutsu sorcerers. He saved people like Yuji Itadora, Yuta Okkotsu, and became Megumi Fushiguro's endorser.

  • Grade: Special Grade
  • Techniques:
    • Limitless - This technique grants its users nigh-absolute control over space through cursed energy manipulation at an atomic level
    • Cursed Techniques: Blue, Red, and Hollow Purple
  • Domain Expansion
    • Infinite Void - It creates a metaphysical space that causes the victims to receive all kinds of stimuli and information endlessly, restraining their thought processes and actions.
